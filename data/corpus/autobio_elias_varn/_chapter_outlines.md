## Writing Notes

### Voice Consistency
- First person, retrospective
- Formal but accessible 18th-century style
- Technical when describing inventions
- Personal when describing relationships
- Philosophical when reflecting on meaning

### Pacing
- Early chapters: more detail on formative experiences
- Middle chapters: balance action and reflection
- Later chapters: more philosophical, less event-driven
- Appendix: documentary reference

### Cross-References
- Each chapter should reference at least one element from a previous chapter
- Characters should reappear appropriately
- Inventions should build on previous work
- Timeline should remain consistent
